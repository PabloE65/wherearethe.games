This retro-looking Chilean Game is betting on Kickstarter

The Team™
Oct 28, 2024

A trio of Chilean indie developers has launched a Kickstarter campaign for their game, BeDo, a pixel-art space game that combines classic platforming with cosmic exploration. In BeDo, you glide through 40 colorful levels, collecting "Dough of Vital Creation" scattered across the universe to restore balance and battle the dark force known as the Slumber.

Players can move freely in all directions, dodge enemies, and explore hidden areas, all backed by an atmospheric, nostalgia-filled soundtrack. With its retro vibe and unique floating mechanic, BeDo promises a fresh, challenging experience for fans of classic games and space adventures alike. Keep an eye out for this Chilean gem!
